# Share My Tabs — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2026

## What data does Share My Tabs access?

Share My Tabs reads the URLs and titles of your open browser tabs when you click the extension icon. This is required to build the list of tabs you can export.

## Does Share My Tabs collect or transmit any data?

**No.** All data stays entirely on YOUR device. Share My Tabs does not:

- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking
- Store any data beyond the current session
- Access browsing history

## What is the exported file?

When you export tabs, a JSON file is saved locally to your computer. You choose whether and how to share that file. Tab Share has no involvement after the file is created.

## Permissions

- **tabs** — Used solely to read the URLs and titles of your currently open tabs for export. No other use.
